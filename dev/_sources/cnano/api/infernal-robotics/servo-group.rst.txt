ServoGroup
==========


.. type:: krpc_InfernalRobotics_ServoGroup_t

   A group of servos, obtained by calling :func:`krpc_InfernalRobotics_ServoGroups`
   or :func:`krpc_InfernalRobotics_ServoGroupWithName`. Represents the "Servo Groups"
   in the InfernalRobotics UI.
